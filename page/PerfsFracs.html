To produce oil

To produce a well, you need to perforate an interval to allow it to flow to see if it flows oil and how much oil flows

Perforating wells

This old oil field has +100 wells in the vicinity, of which 90 have been perforated totalling 11,897m of perforations. There are 17 different types of facies (rock types), 10 different geological ages.

Fracking wells

To improve production, a zone is fracked which can result in 2-20 times improvement in productivity. Fifteen wels were fracked totalling 1128m and these data were analysed to detect which parameters and zones produce the best results when fracked.

Well cross section - three wells

Commingling of production makes identifying how much each zone difficult

When there are multiple producing zones in the same well bore, it is impossible to differentiate from where the production comes from. Such as a well has three zones, we do not know if each zone has an equal or unequal amount of production. Machine Learning on the preforated and fracked zones was used to try and detect which well parameters and zones produce better or worse.
